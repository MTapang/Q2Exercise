Sydney Trip 2022

A glimpse of our first trip abroad during the pandemic and reuniting with my brothers after four months.

Sydney Tower Eye is the highest structure in Sydney, Australia and it is absolutely one of the tourist spots since it offers a skywalk on the observation deck and a buffet with an alluring view of the city.
A stolen picture of my family while wandering the cold weather and streets of Sydney.
Sydney is also well-known for the construction of the Darling Harbour Bridge. Today, this bridge is used for the convenience of the transportation of trains.
Of course, we also went to an amusement park; Luna Park. The bumper car was my favorite part since it was only me and my siblings who tried it and the amusement park offered various and enthralling rides which would definitely make you fall in line to try each ride.
The famous Apple Store located in the city which made me impressive with its astonishing architecural design.
We also visited one of the most well-known performing arts centre. Sydney Opera House To give you a trivia, the roof of the opera house gives mesmerizing colors and different designs which represents its traditional celebration of Vivid Sydney.
Last day spent in Sydney. If only I could stay, I would. Truly, a July to always remember. Sydney will always be my second home, so here's to more Sydney trips with my favorite people.
